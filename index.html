Open Stats Lab

Independent experiments with open data

An independent workbench of projects built on Statistics Canada's open data (plus FRED, ABS, and Statistics Finland where cross-country comparison needs them). Everything is static, pipeline-generated, and open — see the repository. Open Stats Lab is a personal project and is not affiliated with, or endorsed by, Statistics Canada; official data always lives at statcan.gc.ca.
